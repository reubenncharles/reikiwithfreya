Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese energy healing technique that promotes relaxation, reduces stress and anxiety, and encourages healing. It's based on the idea that an unseen "life force energy" flows through us. If one's life force energy is low, then we are more likely to get sick or feel stressed, and if it is high, we are more capable of being happy and healthy.

What does a Reiki session feel like?

Most people report feeling deeply relaxed during a Reiki session. You might experience sensations of warmth, tingling, or a gentle pulsing where my hands are placed or hovering. Some clients feel a release of tension, while others might see colors or images. It's also completely normal to simply feel a deep sense of relaxation.

Do I need to remove clothing for a Reiki session?

No, Reiki is performed with you fully clothed. I recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothes to help you relax during the session.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some people benefit from a single session when addressing a specific issue, while others prefer regular sessions as part of their ongoing wellness routine. After your first session, we can discuss what might be most beneficial for you.

Is Reiki a substitute for medical treatment?

No, Reiki is a complementary therapy that works alongside conventional medical treatment. It is not a substitute for consulting with healthcare professionals or following medical advice.
